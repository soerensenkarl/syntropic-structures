Vision

  • Our mission is to create technology that generates and builds physical structures for humans. Collapsing the gap between human imagination and buildings.
  • Allowing humans to thrive and create sustainably on our planet - in quality buildings that are built, deconstructed - and transformed autonomously by invisible technology.
  • Structures generated by a design philosophy of Elegance, Efficiency, and Economy.1

1 David P. Billington, The Tower and the Bridge: The New Art of Structural Engineering (1983).
